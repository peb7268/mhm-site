SEO: Search engine optimization

SEO is the process of making your site attractive to search engines. It is the process of aligning your site and your business with the terms that potential customers are using to look for businesses like you. SEO is not a magic bullet that will make your P&L explode overnight. It is an essential. It puts you on equal footing with your competition and sets you up for the possibility of being found.
